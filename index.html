About Me

Greetings! I'm a California native who loves to explore scenic hiking trails, have a good back and forth tennis rally, expand my mind through podcasts, and, last but not least, hone my craft in all things web development.

My first interest in web development came when I had to write code to make a static ball roll continuously starting from the left side of the screen to the right. I was amazed at how a few lines of code can have such a powerful effect. I couldn’t wait to build even more compelling things! Further, I loved solving problems logically which naturally fits my personality. With a prior history of being good in math along with being a CPA, I also had transferrable skills towards web development.

Eventually, this led me to join an intensive Full Stack Web Development program at Thinkful to prepare me for a Web Development career. Through 40-50 hours a week dedication to the curriculum, I have gained good ground in HTML/CSS, Javascript, and the MERN stack. I have built three polished Full Stack Web Applications along with other projects you can peruse below. To this day, I am continuously learning, solving algorithms, and seeking out new projects to work on. Please see my Contact section for any work related inquiries.
